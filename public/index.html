Decision Intelligence Overlay: Profit Leak Mitigation

⚠️ The Problem

High-volume products ("GigaCharge Wireless Charger" and "NeoSound Earbuds") are showing net losses (Profit Leakage) despite representing 32% of units sold.

🔍 Root Cause

Average discounts on these products exceed 42% because of automated markdown codes. Price margins cannot support cost + shipping.

📈 Business Impact

Company loses $6.20 on every "GigaCharge Wireless Charger" order, washing out profits generated from the "BaristaPro Espresso Maker".

🛠️ Recommended Action

Instantly replace flat 40%+ discounts with multi-item bundles (e.g. "Buy a Phone Cover, Get 15% off Charger"). Revise shipping policy to charge shipping on discounted items.

Decision Intelligence Overlay: Regional Delivery & Drop-off Correlation

"North America West" sales declined by 48% from July to December 2025, while acquisition sign-ups remained flat.

Decision Intelligence Overlay: Cohort Decay & Retention Uplift

⚠️ The Problem

A sharp retention drop-off occurs in Month 1 (from 100% down to 18-22% across all monthly cohorts), leading to a flat long-term curve.

🔍 Root Cause

A lack of immediate post-purchase onboarding or contextual email offers, leaving customers with no immediate incentive to return.

📈 Business Impact

Acquiring customers is not generating long-term equity. The LTV/CAC ratio is currently low (1.4x), whereas healthy models operate at >3x.

🛠️ Recommended Action

Deploy a "First-Purchase Thank You" automation offering custom accessory suggestions for the purchased product. Establish a VIP membership tier with free shipping on all orders over $25.
